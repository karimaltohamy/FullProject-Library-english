Copy right

riwaya library

The management of a novel library is aware of the danger of violating copyright and intellectual property rights in any book, economically and legally, on the individual and society. Therefore, we are trying hard to control the published books, stop all violating books, and leave them for evaluation and review only, without the possibility of downloading or previewing the book.

Classification of books in a novel library according to the publishing license:

1- Books under the public domain license, and no one can deny them, such as books (The Holy Qur’an - Sahih al-Bukhari - Sahih Muslim - Ultra books such as Ibn Taymiyyah books - Mtib Ibn al-Qayyim - Ibn Sina books), which are very old books and are in the library, and this category represents 20% of the published books At the library

2- Old books of 50 years or more with a Creative Commons license, such as books written by (Naguib Mahfouz - Abbas Al-Akkad - Muhammad Al-Ghazali - Al-Manfaluti - and others), and this category represents 30% of the books published in the library

3- The books of the authors are the ones who registered in the library, and their identity was confirmed by communicating with them, and they offer their knowledge and writings for free, so that everyone can benefit. This category represents 15% of the books published in the library.

4- The authors’ books allowed them to be published after communicating with them, and the other authors allowed them to be published, and they mentioned that in their lectures or through their accounts, and this category represents 5% of the published books

5- Copyright books are reserved and cannot be downloaded or are available as a web page for review and evaluation. Publishing houses do not agree to publish them. These include new books, and this category represents 20% of the books in the library.

- We would like to point out that all these books are constantly reviewed and the violators are suspended without hesitation - The option to report the violating book is given to be stopped, and we received many reports and were stopped message and values riwaya library

message and values riwaya lib1- Contributing to the advancement and development of Arab societies and spreading culture and awareness rary

4- Facilitating the publication of books and communication between authors, readers, and institutions

5- Facilitate the marketing, sale and distribution of electronic and paper Arabic books

6- Preserving the publishing rights of Arabic electronic and paper authors
